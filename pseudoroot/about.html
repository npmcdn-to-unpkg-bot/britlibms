About Browsing the Catalogue of Illuminated Manuscripts

This interface to the British Library's collection of illuminated manuscripts was created by Thomas Evans as a final project for MSc Computer Science at University College London.

It aims to be more "generous" than the old interface so that you can discover logical groupings of manuscripts.
